# Epidemiology AI - Goals Framework

## 1. Outcome Goals

### Primary Outcomes

- **Early Detection Achievement**: Detect disease outbreaks 2-4 weeks earlier than traditional surveillance methods
- **Accuracy Target**: Achieve 90% accuracy in outbreak predictions
- **Response Time Improvement**: Reduce public health response time by 50-70%
- **False Positive Control**: Maintain false positive alert rate below 10%

### Secondary Outcomes

- **User Adoption**: Achieve 80% adoption rate among target public health officials within 6 months of launch
- **Resource Optimization**: Improve resource allocation efficiency by 30%
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction**: Achieve 4.5/5 satisfaction rating from users
- **Lives Impact**: Contribute to preventing large-scale disease outbreaks
